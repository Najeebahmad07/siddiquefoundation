ABOUT US

Siddique Foundation Madhopur is a religious education and aid organization. Who works for orphans, poor, widows and the poor. Marriages of poor orphan girls, assistance to widows, treatment of poor and destitute people and education of theology is given by establishing schools of religion in the poor settlements of East Champaran. Siddique Foundation Educational And Welfare Trust is made on the day of 03/03/2021. Siddique Foundation is made by Masaudul Hasan S/O Mohammad alam age about 37 year's resident of village Madhopur Madhumalat P.O Madhopur P.s Turkauliya, District East Champaran Bihar.
